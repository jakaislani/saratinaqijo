Louis Riel - Rebellion, Death &amp; Facts

12 May 2024, 00:00

(1844-1885) Who Was Louis Riel?Louis Riel was born in modern-day Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. Following his early training for priesthood, Riel headed a 1869-70 rebellion against the transfer of Métis land from the Hudson's Bay Company to the Dominion of Canada, leading to the formation of the province of Manitoba. Exiled by the Canadian government and institutionalized in the 1870s, Riel returned to Métis territory in 1884 to lead another uprising. After surrendering, Riel was found guilty of treason and hanged on November 16, 1885, in Regina, Canada.
