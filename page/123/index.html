How do you fix runny whipped cream

20 May 2024, 00:00

How do you thicken runny whipped cream? To fix whipped cream that is runny, try whisking it again with a half teaspoon of cream of tartar or with cooled unflavored gelatin to help stabilize the delicate topping, especially in hot weather. What do you do if whipping cream won’t whip? Chill it. In fact, chill not only the cream but also the bowl and even the whisk. Place it all with the remainder of the whipping cream you were whipping in the refrigerator, and chill for at least 30 minutes.
