    El DeBarge Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

    Eldra Patrick "El" DeBarge (born June 4, 1961) is an American R&B/Pop falsetto singer and was the focal point and lead singer of family group DeBarge throughout the early to mid-1980s. As a solo artist, he is best known for the hits "Who's Johnny" and "Love Always" and for his collaborations with Tone Loc, Quincy Jones, Fourplay, and DJ Quik. DeBarge is a three-time Grammy nominee. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
