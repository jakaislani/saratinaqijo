    Television actress who is predominantly recognized for her performance as Ekim on the series Duy Beni. She has also appeared on Alparslan: Büyük Selçuklu and Benim Adim Melek. She has appeared in over 100 episodes of TV throughout her career. 

    Before Fame

    She made her professional acting debut on a 2018 episode of Çukurdakiler. 

    Trivia

    She starred in Persona as Süveyda. In 2019, she expanded out of television with a performance in the feature film Özgür Dünya. 

    Family Life

    She was born in Turkey. She has a pet french bulldog.

    Associated With

    She was cast alongside Berk Hakman on Duy Beni.
