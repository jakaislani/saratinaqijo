    Offset's Fight With 42 Dugg Led To Lil Baby Beef, Wack 100 Claims

    Wack 100 says that Offset's feud with Lil Baby can be traced back to a heated dice game with 42 Dugg. Wack recalled the incident on his100 ENTshow, revealing that the two came to blows before he intervened. He explained that the two were putting up thousands of dollars on each roll.

    01 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Wack 100 says that Offset's feud with Lil Baby can be traced back to a heated dice game with 42 Dugg. Wack recalled the incident on his 100 ENT show, revealing that the two came to blows before he intervened. He explained that the two were putting up thousands of dollars on each roll.

    “I was there, I watched it. Got in the middle of it, stopped it,” he said. “It would have blazed that night. Over dice. If it wasn’t for Wack, that n***a would have been in the morgue that night. I told Dugg, ‘You all the way wrong.'" At one point, Dugg wanted to leave but the Migos rapper remarked: “As long as it’s my dice, you got to shoot," according to Wack. "But Dugg walked out of the studio, leading to a confrontation between the two. [Offset] did it like a gangsta, went in Dugg’s pockets, took his money and fired on Dugg. One-on-one. Woomp, bow! ‘You got me f*cked up.'"

    Wack says he had to intervene at that point to prevent an all-out brawl. "I grabbed 42 Dugg, told his little homies, ‘Y’all put that up. Offset, go back in the studio.’ Offset had his little goons with him, they wasn’t budging. I said, ‘Offset, you know there’s gon’ be hell to pay come the morning.’ We were at QC studios." He added: "This was like right before Dugg sparked; he wouldn’t have been around for his spark. Offset and some of his people would’ve been dead. QC studios would’ve been under investigation."
